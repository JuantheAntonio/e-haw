E-System for Harmonizing Actions and Welfare

is a web-based reporting platform for Barangay Camputhaw, Cebu City. It enables residents and officials to digitally report community issues, ranging from environmental hazards like illegal dumping to public safety concerns.

50+ Issues Resolved  |  10-Minute Average Report Time  |  24/7 Monitoring

How It Works

Step 1: Capture

Take a photo or describe the issue.

Step 2: Submit

Create a message stating your concern and send it via the portal.

Step 3: Resolve

Barangay officials review the report and take the necessary actions.
